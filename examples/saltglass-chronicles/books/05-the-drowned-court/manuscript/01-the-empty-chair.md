# The Empty Chair

The Room of Want was colder than Wren remembered.

Dorian Vell pushed the door open and shook the rain from his coat. "You all waited for me," he said, and dropped into the nearest chair.

Wren managed a tired smile. She had not slept since Halden Brooke's funeral, and the grief still sat behind her ribs like a swallowed stone.

"We have news," said Corin Sedgewick, mud to his knees. It was Corin Sedgewick who had ridden hardest from the coast, and his face was grey with it. "They are saying Vespera Locke killed Perrin Marsh at the crossing."

Wren did not want to beleive it. Perrin had promised her he would be carefull.

Odile Sarkany's hair was fire-red in the lamplight as she leaned over the map, and for a long moment no one dared to speak.
