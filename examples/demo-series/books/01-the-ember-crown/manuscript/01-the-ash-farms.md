# The Ash Farms

Aria walked the terrace rows before dawn, sickles crossed at her back. Below the
caldera wall, Emberfall glowed like a banked coal.

Kael waited at the gate with two horses and his usual scowl. "The Ember Witch
rides again," he said.

"Don't call me that," Aria said, and swung into the saddle.

They began riding with Aria's copper-red long hair flowing behind her, and Kael's short brown hair gently waving in the wind.
